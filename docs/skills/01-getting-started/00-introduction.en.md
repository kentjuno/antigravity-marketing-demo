# 🚀 GETTING STARTED: THE ANTIGRAVITY MARKETING KIT (AKM) PLAYBOOK

Welcome to the **Antigravity Marketing Kit (AKM)** - an ecosystem of 127+ AI Skills designed to turn an absolute beginner into an automated cash-printing machine.

This document is your **BLUEPRINT**. Facing over 100 commands (stored in the `skills/En/` directory) can feel overwhelming. Don't panic. Read these rules carefully and strictly follow the Build Order below.

---

## 🛑 2 SURVIVAL RULES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

1. **NEVER Skip Steps:** Many beginners jump straight to the `/akm:copywriter` command and say, "Write me a sales post." This is a fatal mistake. You will get generic, vanilla output. You MUST go from Step 1 (Research) -> Step 2 (Funnel Architecture) -> Step 3 (Copywriting).
2. **Always Provide Context:** AI cannot read your mind. When entering a command, always feed it the details: What are you selling? Who is your target audience? What is the price point? What is your competitor's fatal flaw? The deeper the context, the deadlier the AI.

---

## 🗺️ THE PERFECT BUILD ORDER

Below is the ultimate blueprint to launch any product or service from scratch. Execute these commands in this exact order:

### 🧠 PHASE 1: THE BRAIN (RESEARCH & STRATEGY)
*Don't write a single word of copy until you've gathered intelligence.*

*   **Step 1 - Market Reconnaissance:** Use `/akm:marketing-research`. Force the AI to dig deep and find the bleeding-neck pain points of your target audience (e.g., Office workers in Markham).
*   **Step 2 - Competitor Takedown:** Use `/akm:competitor`. Task the AI with analyzing your biggest rival, finding their fatal weaknesses, and turning them into your unique selling proposition (USP).
*   **Step 3 - The Bait (Lead Magnet):** Use `/akm:free-tool-strategy`. Have the AI conceptualize a "No-brainer Freebie" (e.g., an E-book, a 50% discount code, a template) to ethically bribe customers for their phone numbers.
*   **Step 4 - Funnel Architecture (Crucial):** Use `/akm:funnel`. Map out the exact customer journey: Ad Click -> Claim Freebie -> First Purchase -> Irresistible Upsell.

### ✍️ PHASE 2: THE WEAPONRY (COPYWRITING & DESIGN)
*With the funnel mapped out, it's time to forge the hooks.*

*   **Step 5 - Facebook Ads (The Hook):** Use `/akm:copywriter`. Command the AI to apply the AIDA formula (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action) to write razor-sharp, scroll-stopping copy that triggers emotional buying.
*   **Step 6 - Short-Form Video (TikTok/Reels):** Use `/akm:content-creator`. Generate A/V scripts (3-second hook, body, CTA) that maximize audience retention.
*   **Step 7 - Visual Engineering:** Use `/akm:ai-artist` (for hyper-realistic/cyberpunk aesthetics) or `/akm:logo-design` (for minimalist, premium branding). People buy with their eyes first!
*   **Step 8 - Psychological Manipulation:** Use `/akm:marketing-psychology`. Embed psychological triggers (e.g., The Decoy Effect, FOMO) directly into your pricing tables and menus.

### 💰 PHASE 3: PULLING THE TRIGGER (ADS & CONVERSIONS)
*The guns are loaded. Time to pay Mark Zuckerberg to find your customers.*

*   **Step 9 - Scaling Ads:** Use `/akm:ads-management`. The AI acts as your Media Buyer, guiding you on targeting, budget allocation, and A/B testing to ensure you don't burn cash.
*   **Step 10 - Lead Qualification:** Use `/akm:lead-qualifier`. Don't reply manually. Use AI to build a 3-question sequence that filters out tire-kickers and identifies high-ticket buyers.
*   **Step 11 - The Relentless Follow-Up:** Did they grab the discount but ghost you? Use `/akm:email-wizard`. Generate an automated 3-day email/SMS sequence to relentlessly follow up and close the deal.
*   **Step 12 - Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO):** Use `/akm:campaign-debugger`. If your Cost Per Lead (CPL) is skyrocketing, feed the metrics to the AI. It will diagnose whether your image, copy, or landing page button is broken.

### ⚙️ PHASE 4: THE ENGINE (AUTOMATION & TECH)
*Too much traffic crashing your site? Hand it over to the Tech team.*

*   **Step 13 - Bulletproof Infrastructure:** Use `/akm:software-architect` to design a high-availability backend (e.g., Redis, PostgreSQL) to prevent server crashes during viral spikes.
*   **Step 14 - Fullstack Development:** Use `/akm:fullstack`, `/akm:react`, or `/akm:backend-development` to write the actual code for your web apps and payment integrations.
