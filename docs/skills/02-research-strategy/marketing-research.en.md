# Command: /akm:marketing-research

## 🎯 What it does (Purpose)
This skill is your automated Market Research Analyst. It gathers real-time data by scraping the internet, aggregating customer reviews, analyzing menus, tracking competitors, and giving you a bird's-eye view of any brand or niche industry.

## 🛠️ How to use (Syntax)
**Syntax:** `/akm:marketing-research [Brand / Topic to research]`

**Examples:**
- `/akm:marketing-research Analyze the healthy beverage niche in downtown Toronto.`
- `/akm:marketing-research Aggregate customer reviews for Pho Kent Juno in Markham to find strengths and weaknesses.`

## 💡 Real-world Scenario (Use Case)

**Context:** You want to pull the "Pho Kent Juno" project out of theory and into the real world. You need to know exactly what a restaurant at 4500 Steeles Ave E, Markham is selling, its aesthetic, and what customers are complaining about.

**Command Executed:** 
`/akm:marketing-research Conduct real-world market research on Pho Kent Juno located at 4500 Steeles Ave E, Markham. Focus on the Menu, Customer Reviews, and overall Vibe of the restaurant.`

**Expected Output:**
The AI will browse the internet and return a hard-hitting intelligence report:
1. **Actual Positioning:** "Modern Casual Premium" (Clean, bright, modern) rather than ultra-luxury Fine Dining.
2. **Signature Menu:** Short-rib Pho, Premium Crab Sticky Rice, Cha Ca La Vong.
3. **Review Analysis (Customer Insights):**
   - *Pros:* Deep, flavorful broth; beautiful interior; authentic regional dishes.
   - *Cons:* Service can be slow during rush hours; portion sizes for certain sides (like fish sauce wings) are small for the price.
   - *Hack:* 5-10% discount when paying with Cash.

## ⚠️ Pro Tips
- **Review Insights are a Goldmine:** When you use this skill to analyze competitor reviews, you uncover exact customer "Pain points." Use those exact pain points as the primary hook in your ad copy.
- **Real Data = Real Action:** Unlike brainstorming (which is ideation), `marketing-research` forces the AI to connect to the internet. Always ask the AI to cite its sources so you can fact-check the intelligence if needed.
