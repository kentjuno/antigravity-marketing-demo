# Command: /akm:copywriter

## 🎯 What it does (Purpose)
Many beginners confuse *Content* with *Copywriting*. Content is meant to attract, entertain, and educate (e.g., TikTok videos, Blogs). **Copywriting**, however, exists for one singular, ruthless purpose: **TO SELL AND CLOSE DEALS**. 

The `/akm:copywriter` skill transforms the AI into a Direct-Response Sales Assassin. It utilizes legendary copywriting frameworks (like AIDA, PAS, BAB) to agitate customer pain points, spike desire, and psychologically force them to pull out their credit card or hand over their email address.

## 🛠️ How to use (Syntax)
**Syntax:** `/akm:copywriter [Type of Ad/Copy] for [Product/Campaign]`

**Examples:**
- `/akm:copywriter Write a Facebook Ad using the PAS formula to sell a hair-loss serum for postpartum mothers.`
- `/akm:copywriter Write a scroll-stopping Headline for a landing page selling a high-ticket English course.`

## 💡 Real-world Scenario (Use Case)

**Context:** In Wave 1, you engineered the "Signature Combo" campaign. Now you need to launch Facebook Ads to carpet-bomb the Markham area. You need a lethal piece of Ad Copy. If the copy is weak, users will just scroll past. You need an ad engineered using the **AIDA** framework (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action).

**Command Executed:** 
`/akm:copywriter Act as a high-converting Direct Response Copywriter. Write a Facebook Ad for the "Kent Juno Signature Combo" campaign. Requirements:
1. Strictly follow the AIDA framework.
2. The Headline must trigger intense curiosity and FOMO.
3. The Offer: Free Premium Herbal Iced Tea for customers who know the "Secret Code."
4. Tone: Luxurious but highly persuasive and urgent.`

**Expected Output:**
The AI generates a highly-structured, irresistible ad:

> **[ATTENTION - Stop the scroll]**
> 🛑 DON'T LOOK FOR THIS ON THE MENU. YOU WON'T FIND IT.
> 
> **[INTEREST - Build curiosity]**
> You think you've tasted everything Pho Kent Juno has to offer? Think again. Our Head Chef just locked an "Exclusive Privilege" in the vault, reserved strictly for true culinary insiders in Markham.
> Introducing: The "Signature Combo" – An unlisted pairing we’ve never publicized.
> 
> **[DESIRE - Stoke the craving]**
> When the melt-in-your-mouth Kagoshima A5 Wagyu meets our boiling 48-hour heritage broth... the only thing that can elevate the experience is an ice-cold, masterfully brewed Premium Herbal Tea.
> What if we told you we are giving away this Premium Herbal Tea ABSOLUTELY FREE on your next visit?
> 
> **[ACTION - Force the click]**
> We are only releasing 50 VIP Passes this week!
> 👉 Click the [SEND MESSAGE] button below to let our Chatbot grant you the "Secret Code."
> Whisper the code to our cashier to unlock this hidden combo. Do not wait!

## ⚠️ Pro Tips
- **Frameworks are King:** Never just tell the AI "Write an ad." Always specify a framework. If you sell a product that solves a painful problem (Acne cream, orthopedic mattresses), command the AI to use the **PAS** formula (Problem, Agitate, Solution).
- **Split-Test Headlines:** The headline dictates 80% of your ad's success. Always append: *"Write the body copy, but provide 5 radically different Headlines so I can A/B test them."*
