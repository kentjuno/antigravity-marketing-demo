# Command: /akm:ai-artist

## 🎯 What it does (Purpose)
For beginners: In advertising, the Visual dictates 70% of the Click-Through Rate. If you don't know Photoshop and can't afford a professional food photographer, the `/akm:ai-artist` skill is your savior.

It acts as your elite **AI Illustrator**. Its mission is to transform your text-based ideas into stunning visual imagery. You can command it to render any aesthetic: from hyper-realistic (Photorealistic) photography, to 3D Pixar styles, Vintage aesthetics, or futuristic Cyberpunk vibes.

## 🛠️ How to use (Syntax)
**Syntax:** `/akm:ai-artist Draw [Subject of the image] in [Desired Style]`

**Examples:**
- `/akm:ai-artist Draw a photorealistic image of a steaming cup of salted coffee on a wooden table in the morning light.`
- `/akm:ai-artist Draw a 3D Pixar-style illustration of a cat wearing a chef's apron.`

## 💡 Real-world Scenario (Use Case)

**Context:** You want to print a massive, high-impact Poster to display outside Pho Kent Juno, and use it as a Facebook Cover Photo. You refuse to use a boring, standard photo of a Pho bowl like every other restaurant. You want something "Artistic" that instantly grabs Gen-Z's attention using a Cyberpunk aesthetic (moody future, neon lights).

**Command Executed:** 
`/akm:ai-artist Draw an artistic illustration featuring a steaming bowl of A5 Wagyu Pho. 
Requirements: 
- Style: Cyberpunk, with vibrant Red and Blue Neon lights reflecting off the broth.
- Setting: A futuristic noodle shop in Markham; it is raining heavily outside the window. 
- Atmosphere: Cinematic, hyper-realistic details.`

**Expected Output:**
The AI renders and returns a breathtaking image directly to your screen:
*(You will see a massive bowl of Pho with vibrant red Wagyu marbling, the steam swirling into the neon blue and red lights reflecting from the rainy Markham streets).*

👉 **Application:** Immediately drop this image into the *Quiz: It's raining on a Saturday night, what do you crave?* campaign from Wave 1. Your Click-Through Rate (CTR) will skyrocket!

## ⚠️ Pro Tips
- **The Prompt Formula:** To generate elite images, strictly follow this structure: `[Primary Subject] + [Setting/Background] + [Art Style] + [Lighting/Color Grading]`.
- **Lighting is King:** Do not just describe the bowl. Force the AI to use lighting keywords like: *Cinematic lighting*, *Volumetric lighting*, or *Golden hour*. Lighting is what makes an image look expensive.
