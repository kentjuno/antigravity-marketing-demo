# Command: /akm:ads-management

## 🎯 What it does (Purpose)
For beginners: To pour fuel into your Funnel, you cannot just post on your Facebook page and pray (because organic reach is practically dead). You must pay to play (Facebook Ads, Google Ads). 

The `/akm:ads-management` skill transforms the AI into an elite **Media Buyer**. It won't physically click buttons in your Ads Manager (you still need your credit card for that), but it will architect a surgical **Ad Setup Blueprint** down to the exact click: What objective should you choose? Who exactly should you target? And how do you split a $100/day budget to acquire leads at the lowest possible cost?

## 🛠️ How to use (Syntax)
**Syntax:** `/akm:ads-management [Ad Platform] for [Campaign Name]`

**Examples:**
- `/akm:ads-management Blueprint a Google Search Ads campaign structure for an emergency plumbing service in Toronto.`
- `/akm:ads-management Outline how to allocate a $500 budget on Facebook Ads to sell winter apparel.`

## 💡 Real-world Scenario (Use Case)

**Context:** You've finalized the viral "Flaming Wok" TikTok video (Wave 2) and your AIDA copy. Now it's time to burn some cash and run Facebook Ads for the "Signature Combo" campaign to drag Markham residents into Pho Kent Juno. However, the Facebook Ads Manager is a labyrinth of complex settings. If you choose the wrong objective, you will burn your budget into ashes with zero results. You need the AI to hold your hand.

**Command Executed:** 
`/akm:ads-management Act as a Senior Facebook Media Buyer. Architect the exact Facebook Ads setup for the "Signature Combo" campaign (giving away Free Herbal Tea with Pho) for Pho Kent Juno. Budget: $100 CAD/day. Requirements:
1. Which Campaign Objective must I select to harvest the most VIP coupons?
2. How do I configure the Target Audience (Age, Geo-radius, Interests) to accurately snipe "affluent foodies" in Markham?
3. How should I allocate the budget to A/B test 2 different ad sets?`

**Expected Output:**
The AI delivers a clinical, step-by-step technical blueprint:

> **⚙️ FACEBOOK ADS SETUP BLUEPRINT: THE SIGNATURE COMBO**
> Budget: $100 CAD / Day | Placements: Facebook & Instagram Feeds
> 
> **1. Campaign Level:**
> - *Objective:* DO NOT select "Engagement." You must select **"Messages" or "Lead Generation"**. Our sole objective is to force users to interact with our Chatbot to claim the VIP Code.
> 
> **2. Ad Set Level (A/B Testing 2 Audiences):**
> Split the $100 CAD into two $50/day Ad Sets to identify the cheapest Cost Per Lead (CPL).
> - **Ad Set 1 (Broad Targeting):** Location: 10km radius around the Markham restaurant. Age: 25 - 55. Zero Interest targeting. Let the Facebook AI algorithm hunt for the buyers.
> - **Ad Set 2 (Luxury/Affluent):** Location: Same. Age: 25 - 55. Interests: "Fine Dining", "Steakhouse", "Wagyu", "Luxury lifestyle". 
> 
> **3. Ad Level:**
> - *Creative:* Deploy the "Flaming Wok" video and the AIDA-structured copy.
> - *Call To Action (CTA):* Hardcode the button to **"Send Message"**.

## ⚠️ Pro Tips
- **Kill the Audience Network:** Always ask the AI: *"Should I disable the Audience Network placement?"* The answer is always YES. Displaying your premium ads on garbage mobile games burns money.
- **The 3-Day Rule:** If your Cost Per Lead (CPL) is higher than $5 CAD after 3 days, instantly kill the campaign and deploy the `/akm:campaign-debugger` skill to figure out why you are bleeding cash.
